How It Works

Automated Review Requests After Every Job

When a job is marked complete, your customer receives a text with a direct link to leave a Google review. Timed for the moment they're happiest with the work — not three weeks later when they've forgotten your name. Follow-up messages go out if they don't respond the first time.

AI Chat Agent for After-Hours Leads

Most homeowners search for landscapers at night. If someone finds your profile at 9 PM and sends a message, the AI agent responds instantly — captures their info, qualifies the lead, and books them into your pipeline. No more waking up to missed inquiries that already called someone else.

Smart Review Filtering

Before sending a customer to Google, the system asks how their experience was. Happy customers go straight to your review page. Unhappy ones get routed to a private feedback form so you can handle the issue before it becomes a public one-star. Protects your rating while you grow it.

Pipeline Visibility Without Manual Updates

See which jobs triggered review requests, which customers responded, and where your review count stands — all in one dashboard. No spreadsheets, no guessing. You'll know exactly which neighborhoods are generating your strongest social proof and where you've got gaps.
