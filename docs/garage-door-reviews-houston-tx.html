What We Install

How FBS Builds Garage Door Review Automation That Actually Works in Houston

We install a two-part review system directly inside your GoHighLevel CRM. When a job moves to "complete" in your pipeline — whether it's a torsion spring swap in Kingwood or a full door replacement in Sugar Land — the automation fires. First, a satisfaction check goes out to the customer via text. This isn't a review request yet. It's a simple question: how'd we do? That check has to happen before any review link ever gets sent.

If the customer responds positively, they immediately receive a direct link to your Google Business Profile. If they respond negatively, they're routed to a private feedback form that comes straight to you — not to Google, not to Yelp, nowhere public. You get the chance to make it right before it ever becomes a reputation issue. The whole thing runs without your techs or your office staff doing anything beyond closing the job in the system.
