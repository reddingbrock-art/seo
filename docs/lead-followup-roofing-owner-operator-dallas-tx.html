What's Inside the System

AI Lead Capture Agent

When a lead hits your site at 9 PM or calls on a Saturday morning while you're loading shingles, the AI agent responds instantly via chat or voice. It collects the address, describes the issue, and books the estimate — no human needed. Leads that would've bounced to a competitor now stay in your pipeline.

Automated Follow-Up Sequences

Every new lead enters a timed sequence of texts and emails — first touch within minutes, then follow-ups spaced over the next several days. These aren't generic blasts. They're written for roofing, referencing storm damage, insurance claims, and the urgency homeowners actually feel. The system keeps working leads until they book or opt out.

Google Review Automation

After a job is marked complete, the system automatically sends a review request via text with a direct link to your Google Business Profile. Most homeowners will leave a review if you ask at the right moment. This is that moment — timed, automated, and consistent. Your review count climbs without you lifting a finger.

Visual Pipeline & Lead Tracking

See every lead, where it came from, and where it sits in your process — new, estimate scheduled, proposal sent, closed. No spreadsheets. No sticky notes on the dash of your truck. You'll know exactly what's in the funnel when you check your phone between jobs, and nothing slips through because someone forgot to write it down.
